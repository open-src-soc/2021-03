Virtual & Real Life Venues

The original schedule for this 2nd RISC-V Week was early November 2020, and was expected to be an event in real life. Due to ongoing sanitary conditions we had to postpone it until and settled for late March/early April, expecting improvement of the sanitary conditions.

As the pandemic seems to be lasting, we decided to turn it into a virtual event.

However, in order to encourage networking and scientific exchanges between participants, and if the sanitary conditions allow us to do so, three sites will be prepared to open to participants.

Virtual Event

The video system will allow for remote connexion of speakers and attendees for presentations and Q&A sessions. Virtual rooms for ad-hoc chat sessions will also be provided.

Real-Life Venues

Depending on authorizations and sanitary rules, we plan to provide access to actual venues in the three following locations:

  • Rennes: at INRIA Rennes, located on the Campus universtaire de Baulieu, avenue du Maréchal-Leclerc, Rennes (Ille-et-Vilaine).

  • Paris (South West): at Thales Research & Technology, located 1 avenue Augustin-Fresnel, Palaiseau (Essonne). Directions in PDF .

  • Grenoble (downtown, TBD).
