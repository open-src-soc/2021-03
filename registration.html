Resgitration and Covid-19

The “2nd RISC-V Week” will be online and, hopefully, also physical, as we plan to open three venues with limited attendance:

  • Rennes: at INRIA Rennes, located on the Campus universtaire de Baulieu 263 avenue du Maréchal-Leclerc, Rennes (Ille-et-Vilaine).
  • Grenoble: Hôtel Novotel Grenoble Centre, located 7 place Robert-Schuman, Grenoble (Isère).
  • Paris (South West): at Thales Research & Technology, located 1 avenue Augustin-Fresnel, Palaiseau (Essonne).

If you wish to physically attend the conference, please mention the venue of your choice when you register.

Depending on the sanitary conditions and the number of places available, we will individually notify those who will be able to physically attend the conference.
